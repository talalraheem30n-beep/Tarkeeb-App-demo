About Tarkeeb

Turning food visuals into structured cooking knowledge.

🎯 Our Mission

To eliminate "Save Fatigue" by transforming visual food content into structured, actionable kitchen intelligence—helping users instantly understand, recreate, and benefit from any dish they see.

👁️ Our Vision

To become the world’s most intuitive food intelligence platform—where anyone can understand any dish, anywhere, instantly.

🚨 The Problem We Solve

Every day, millions of users:

  • Save recipes from social media
  • Forget to revisit them
  • Lack clear, structured cooking instructions

This creates "Save Fatigue"—a frustrating gap between inspiration and execution.

💡 Our Solution

Tarkeeb bridges this gap by allowing users to:

  • 📸 Scan food images
  • 🧠 Identify dishes instantly
  • 🍳 Get structured recipes
  • 📊 Access ingredient & cooking insights

All in one seamless experience.

⚙️ How Tarkeeb Works

  1. Upload or capture a food image
  2. The system analyzes visual patterns
  3. Identifies the closest matching dish
  4. Generates ingredients, steps, and cooking details
👉 In the full version, this will be powered by AI models like Google AI Studio.

✨ Key Features

📷 Image Recognition 🍽️ Instant Recipes 🧂 Ingredient Breakdown ⏱️ Cooking Time & Difficulty 📊 Nutrition Insights (Planned) 💾 Save & Revisit (Planned)
